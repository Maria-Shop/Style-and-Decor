inSync with you

Award-winning studio inSync design was established in Melbourne Australia with a quest to create Australian made contemporary jewellery that is distinct, whimsical and cleverly constructed.

Led by Iris Saar Isaacs, inSync design maintains a fiercely independent approach to contemporary jewellery, whilst marrying cutting-edge technology with traditional practices to craft contemporary jewellery of exceptional quality.

We are a product-focused studio, launching new designs and colours seasonally. Each piece is designed to last, illustrating a minimalist aesthetic and an artisan finish, providing a timeless alternative to mass-produced jewellery.

LINE COLLECTION
inSync design studio gained its initial reputation and recognition for the whimsical ‘hand drawn’ brooches, earrings and necklaces. Fabricated in 100% hypoallergenic stainless steel and available in either raw stainless steel, brightly coloured powder-coating or 22ct matt gold plating. This range was inspired by the children’s book, ‘Harold and the Purple Crayon’ by Crockett Johnson, in which Harold creates a world around him by sketching with his purple crayon.

PEBBLE COLLECTION
An innovative range of necklaces and earrings made of anodized aluminium, neoprene rubber and stainless steel.

The modular necklace incorporates anodized aluminium ‘stones’ with neoprene rubber lariat. The pebbles are carved out of a solid block, with a channel running along its outer diameter, allowing the rubber to embed perfectly into the metal and create a seamless fusion of colour and texture.

The earrings feature a flat plane that is framed by hypoallergenic stainless steel. Created in four colours and four organic shapes, they are designed to form various combinations based on either shape or colour; making them interchangeable.

FROST COLLECTION
Frost Collection brings together coils of midnight color bespoke nylon tubes held together with hollow Linear cocoons made of sterling silver or matt black nylon. This collection explores the aesthetics of volume, through curves, gaps and negative spaces to create simple, and elegant necklaces and bangles.

The use of curved lines, muted colours, transparency and opaqueness highlight the natural beauty of the materials themselves, and create wearable sculptures that are distinct, versatile, elegant and timeless pieces with a contemporary flair.
